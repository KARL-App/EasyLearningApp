Services

Personalized lessons

We offer you the easiest way to make interactive worksheets. It takes just a few clicks to prepare your resources. Quizzes, match ups, word games and much more. Evaluation time becomes nicer for children when they get funny tests. Use our templates, bring your content and make it more attractive.

Use it on school domain

The most attractive point is that every school can use it on its own domain for free. It can improve communication by providing individual, password-protected accounts for students and parents. It includes ability to post messages an announcements for all students and parents or particularly for groups or specific classes.

Online grade book

Our grade book is completely cloud based, which means there is no software to install and teachers can access data from everywhere. And the best for teachers and students is that it has on all-points grading system or flexible grading system that combines points, letters, grades, checks, checks plus and custom grades.
