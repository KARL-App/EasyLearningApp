Our story

This app started with a teacher`s idea, who's complaining that creating resources for teaching online takes a lot of time. Changing from classic teaching to online teaching was so difficult for many of the teachers for whom technology is not a friendly field. So, we are here to lend a helping hand to every teacher and improve together the education quality.

Easy Learning App

Some reasons for getting our app.

It works as an online teacher and parent portal

You can now adapt your teaching to children`s needs!

Support for multiple education formats (visual and audio)

It can be used on any devices.

SMS Notifications 

You'll get a notification when your class starts or when or when a teacher write an important announcement.
